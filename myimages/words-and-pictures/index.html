Words and Pictures go together. I know that some photographers think that images should stand on their own and some artists believe that art should never be explained. I completely disagree with both of these views and I think that photographs are always enhanced by complementary words. These may comment on how the photographer feels about the images, say something about the location of the image or simply explain why the photographer chose these images for display.
