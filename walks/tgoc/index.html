The TGO Challenge

The TGO Challenge is an annual event where people walk across Scotland from the west to the east coast. They choose their own route and there are various meeting points, such as Braemar, where participants congregate. I’ve taken part in this 4 times (so far) and I hope to be able to do a few more.

I’ve documented my Challenges under a separate heading.
