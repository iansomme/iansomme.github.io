Books

    Tags: 

    I’ve written a number of books during my academic career that I’ve listed under the Books link on my professional pages. Two of these books are widely used and I maintain web sites to support them.

    Software Engineering, 10th Edition. This site supports the 10th edition of my software engineering textbook, which was first published in 1982. The 10th edition was published in 2015. There’s lots of teaching material that can be downloaded from this site.

    Engineering Software Products. I have retired my software engineering textbook because I think that the project-oriented view of software engineering that it presents is less relevant for the development of software products and apps. This, more recent, book focuses on software engineering techniques and methods for developing this type of software.
